Our story

Not flavored. Finished.

Aurora was born from the belief that honey can do more than sweeten. It can soften, round, and transform a rum spirit into something smoother, warmer, and more memorable.

The final profile is formulated and made in Puerto Rico — a refined honey rum created for golden-hour pours, intimate gatherings, and premium cocktail moments.
