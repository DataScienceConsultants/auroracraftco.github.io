The story

Not flavored. Finished.

Aurora is built around a simple idea: honey can do more than sweeten. It can soften, round, and transform a rum spirit into something smoother, warmer, and more memorable.

The rum is selected, then formulated and made in Puerto Rico with a honey-finished profile designed for sipping, cocktails, and golden-hour moments.
